Bony Tony: The Revenge

Play as concrete-shoed Bony Tony and take revenge on the mafia who sunk you in the sea. Use a shotgun to blast yourself to the top of their headquarters and take out the boss.

Bony Tony is a platformer where your main mode of movement is by shooting, which launches your character in the opposite direction.

There are two different weapons that offer two unique ways of movement, a revolver and a shotgun, as well as an extra powerful shot upgrade for the shotgun.
